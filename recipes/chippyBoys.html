Chippy Boys

Home

Simply put because they're simply made, Chippy Boys are potato chips that have been toasted and mixed with cheese and balsamic glaze.

Ingredients

  • Plain
  • ettle-cooked potato chips
  • Gorgonzola
  • store-bought balsamic glaze
  1. spread however many chips you'd like on a parchment-lined sheet tray and top with the crumbled cheese of your choice
  2. Then broil or toast them in the oven or toaster oven until they're golden brown
  3. You may also want to pull them out halfway through toasting and mix them around if you have a lot on the tray to ensure the ones on the top and bottom toast evenly.
  4. When they're toasted to your desired doneness, pull them out and drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  5. Carefully transfer them to a bowl and toss to coat.
  6. Enjoy
